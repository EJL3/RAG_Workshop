OFFICIAL CAMPUS & HOSTEL RULES AND REGULATIONS (2025-2026)

1. CURFEW: All students must be inside the hostel premises by 8:30 PM. Late entry requires prior written permission from the Warden.
2. ATTENDANCE: A minimum of 75% attendance is mandatory to appear for the University Examinations.
3. LIBRARY HOURS: The Central Library is open from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM on weekdays and 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM on Saturdays. It is closed on Sundays.
4. LAB CONDUCT: Students must wear formal attire and carry their ID cards at all times inside the Computer Labs. 
5. INTERNET USAGE: High-speed Wi-Fi is available in the hostels. Usage is restricted for educational purposes between 9:00 AM and 5:00 PM.
6. EMERGENCIES: In case of medical emergencies, contact the Resident Medical Officer (RMO) at Extension 104 or visit the Campus Clinic near the Main Gate.
